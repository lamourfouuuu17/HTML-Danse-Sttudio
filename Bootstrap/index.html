Start your dance journey with the most popular styles

Hip-Hop

A culture and art movement that was created by African Americans, Latino Americans and Caribbean Americans in the Bronx, New York City.

Heels

a dance form that emerged and evolved in the United States and Europe in the late 20th and early 21st centuries. It is named after the women's shoe style, since one of its distinguishing features is the wearing of high-heeled shoes during performance.

Waacking

An extremely expressive, free and musical form of dance that is characterised by arm movements and posing with a strong emphasis on musicality and improvised performance.

House Dance

a style of dance that originated in the late 70's and early 80's from underground clubs in Chicago and New York. House Dance is about freedom, improvisation, and feeling the music.
